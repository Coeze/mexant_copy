Hatred, Discord, Jealousy
Prayer
Worship

We discussed three of them: Hatred, discord and jealousy. We discover that a child of God should not dislike anyone, or have disagreements leading divisions and also that jealousy can be positive or negative. Positive jealousy is to protect one’s right as God is jealous over us- He has His Spirit in us. Negative jealousy lead to envy which is a dissatisfaction about someone’s achievement- we need to rejoice with those are are rejoicing.

Another important point made was: It is natural for human beings to act according to the flesh. So when the temptation comes around you, don’t despair, remember the topic of discussion is: set your mind on the Spirit and you will not fulfil the desires of the flesh. It is natural for unbelievers to act in the flesh- it is normal for them- but for us, we have been given the power of the Holy Spirit to keep us from sinning. We read Romans 6:14. Sin does not dominate us. We have the power to dominate sin, if we allow the power to help us. May the Lord fill you all by His Holy Spirit so you can live the life of God in Jesus name.
